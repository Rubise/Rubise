Back in 2019, I began my journey into the world of web development by diving into custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. What started as a simple curiosity soon turned into a passion for creating seamless, user-friendly websites and applications.

Fast forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working on a variety of projects, from enhancing the digital presence of small businesses to working with a Fiber Optics company with the restoration team, where I developed strong communication and problem-solving skills. My experience spans web development, networking, and even teaching programming languages like C and JavaScript.

Currently, my main focus is on building dynamic, responsive web applications, leveraging technologies like React, Tailwind, Javascript and WordPress. I take pride in crafting code that not only looks great but also performs efficiently. I’m particularly interested in the intersection of design and development, where aesthetics meet functionality.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, watching football/Anime, or spending time with my family. I'm also deeply committed to continuous learning, currently pursuing certifications to expand my expertise in software development and project management.
